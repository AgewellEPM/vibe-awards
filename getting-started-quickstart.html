Let's Try It Right Now

Here's exactly what to type for your first app:

"Build me a daily mood tracker that feels encouraging and supportive. Make it work on mobile and include:

• Simple mood selection (happy, okay, sad, stressed, excited)
• A weekly view showing mood patterns
• Gentle reminders to check in
• Encouraging messages based on mood trends
• Beautiful, calming colors
• Smooth animations that feel satisfying

Make it feel like a caring friend, not a clinical tool. I want users to actually enjoy tracking their mood."

Copy that prompt, paste it into Claude or ChatGPT, and watch AI build you a complete mood tracking app in minutes. Then ask for changes until it feels perfect.
