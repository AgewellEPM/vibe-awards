Our Recommendations

Choose based on your experience level and goals

🌟 Beginners

Start with Claude or ChatGPT. They're the most forgiving and will teach you good prompting habits.

Both explain their decisions well and help you understand what they're building.

💼 Developers

Use Cursor for serious development work. It integrates perfectly with your existing workflow.

Keep Claude or ChatGPT open for design discussions and architecture planning.

🎨 Designers

Try v0 first for beautiful interfaces, then Claude for functionality.

v0 creates stunning designs that feel modern and professional.

⚡ Quick Ideas

Use Bolt when you want to test an idea in under 5 minutes.

Perfect for validating concepts before building them properly.
